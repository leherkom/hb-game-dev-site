What Are We Doing?

We usually try to make games. This can be tiring on your own, so we meet up twice a month to chill and code. We want to provide everyone the opportunity to connect to other developers, showcase their work, search for testers or ask for feedback.

The meet ups are our primary focus, but in future times, you may want to keep your eyes peeled for workshops or game jams as well.

I Want to Showcase Something!

Great! You can present your latest game or talk about any game dev-y topic you find cool, interesting or funny. Let us know at talk[at]hb-game.dev, and we'll be pleased to have you.
